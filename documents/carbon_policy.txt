# Carbon Emissions Policy - GreenLedger Compliance Document

## Overview
This document outlines the carbon emission limits and compliance requirements for all facilities under GreenLedger monitoring.

## Hourly Emission Limits
- Maximum permissible emissions: 500 kg CO2 per hour per facility
- Threshold for immediate alert: 400 kg CO2 per hour
- Exceedance requires immediate investigation and corrective action

## Daily Emission Limits  
- Maximum permissible emissions: 10,000 kg CO2 per day per facility
- Rolling 24-hour average must not exceed 400 kg CO2/hour
- Weekly reports required for facilities approaching 80% of limit

## Production Efficiency Standards
- Target efficiency: 10-15 kg CO2 per production unit
- Warning threshold: > 15 kg CO2 per production unit
- Critical threshold: > 20 kg CO2 per production unit

## Reporting Requirements
1. Real-time monitoring required for all facilities
2. Daily summaries submitted by 06:00 local time
3. Weekly compliance reports due every Monday
4. Annual environmental impact assessment required

## Enforcement
- First violation: Written warning and corrective action plan
- Second violation: Fine of $10,000 and mandatory efficiency audit
- Third violation: Operational restrictions and potential facility closure
