# ESG Compliance Document

## Environmental, Social, and Governance Framework

### Environmental Commitments

#### Carbon Neutrality Goals
- **2025 Target**: 20% reduction from 2020 baseline
- **2030 Target**: 50% reduction from 2020 baseline  
- **2040 Target**: Net zero emissions

#### Scope 1 Emissions (Direct)
- Owned/controlled facilities
- Company vehicles
- On-site fuel combustion

#### Scope 2 Emissions (Indirect)
- Purchased electricity
- Purchased steam/heat/cooling

#### Scope 3 Emissions (Value Chain)
- Supplier emissions (Category 1)
- Transportation (Category 4, 9)
- Employee commuting (Category 7)

### Sustainability Metrics

#### Monthly Tracking
| Metric | Target | Current | Status |
|--------|--------|---------|--------|
| Total CO2 Emissions | < 5000 tons | Track | Monitor |
| Energy Intensity | < 100 kWh/unit | Track | Monitor |
| Renewable Energy % | > 30% | 22% | ⚠️ Behind |
| Waste Diversion | > 75% | 68% | ⚠️ Behind |
| Water Recycling | > 40% | 35% | ⚠️ Behind |

#### Quarterly Reviews
- Board ESG Committee meeting
- Third-party audit of emissions data
- Supplier sustainability assessment

### Social Responsibility

#### Workforce
- Safety incidents target: Zero
- Training hours per employee: 40+/year
- Diversity goal: 40% underrepresented groups

#### Community
- Local hiring: 60% from host communities
- Community investment: 1% of profits
- Public transparency reports

### Governance

#### Board Oversight
- ESG Committee with 3 independent directors
- Quarterly ESG performance reviews
- Annual sustainability report

#### Transparency
- CDP (Carbon Disclosure Project) submission
- TCFD (Task Force on Climate-related Financial Disclosures) aligned reporting
- GRI (Global Reporting Initiative) standards

### Regulatory Compliance

#### Current Regulations
- EPA emissions reporting
- EU ETS (Emissions Trading System)
- California Cap-and-Trade
- SEC climate disclosure (pending)

#### Upcoming Requirements
- CSRD (Corporate Sustainability Reporting Directive)
- ISSB (International Sustainability Standards Board)
- Nature-related financial disclosure (TNFD)

### Audit and Verification

#### Internal Audits
- Monthly: Data quality checks
- Quarterly: Process review
- Annual: Full ESG audit

#### External Verification
- Third-party emissions verification (annually)
- ISO 14001 certification maintenance
- Social audit (SA8000)

### Incident Response

#### Carbon Spill Response
1. Immediate containment
2. Regulatory notification (24 hours)
3. Root cause analysis
4. Corrective action plan
5. Public disclosure if material

#### Compliance Violations
- Minor: Corrective action within 30 days
- Major: Corrective action within 7 days + fine
- Critical: Immediate suspension + regulatory involvement
